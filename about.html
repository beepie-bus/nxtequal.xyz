About

I'm NXT and welcome to my site. Here you'll find things related to my hobbies and fandoms. I'm a Chinese artist living in the UK. I'm trans and xgender, as in nonbinary, and as in the gender of the letter X and what it represents as unknown and other. I'm also cargender, as in my gender is literally car.

Yes I have autism.

My hobbies include art, computers, cars, videogames, RPGs, horror, films and TV shows, food, crafts, cosplay... I consider myself quite a varied person and can talk about anything.

What about my deeper interests? I'm a deep lover of art in all its forms. I'm Buddhist and it's an intrinsic part of me and what I put into my work. As you can see from my site I'm heavily into retro grunge and what I like to call "junkcore". Join my movement!
